 Chocolate Chip Cookies
1 cup of softened butter
1 cup of white sugar + brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
1 teaspoon of baking soda
2 teaspoons of hot water
½ teaspoon of salt
3 cups of all purpose flour
2 cups of chocolate chips
1 cup of chopped walnuts
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Cream together the butter, white sugar and brown sugar until it is smooth
Beat in the eggs one at a time
Then stir in the vanilla
Dissolve the baking soda in hot water
Add to batter along with salt
Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts
Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
Bake for about 10 minutes in a preheated oven or until edges are nicely browned. 


Pumpkin Pie recipe 
1 can pumpkin
1 can of sweetened condensed milk
2 large eggs
1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
½ teaspoon ground ginger
½ teaspoon ground nutmeg 1/2 teaspoon of salt
1 unbaked pie crust
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 245 degrees.
Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium bowl until smooth
Pour into crust
Bake for 15 minutes
Reduce temp to 350 degrees and continue to bake for 35-40 m
Cool
Garnish as desires

Chocolate Covered Strawberries
1 pack of strawberries
Chocolate of your choice that you could melt (dark chocolate, milk chocolate, white chocolate)

Instructions:
Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper
Rinse the packed strawberries
Pat dry with towels
In a microwave, place the chocolate in a bowl and melt it
If you don’t have a microwave, boil some water. Once the water is boiled, take another empty pot and place the chocolate.
Place the chocolate with the pot on top of the boiled water and stir the pot with the chocolate until it has melted.
Dip strawberry in chocolate and then placed on the lined bake sheet
Refrigerate until chocolate is set (for about 30 minutes).
